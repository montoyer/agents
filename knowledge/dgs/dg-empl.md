# DG 

> See `commissioners/` for the Commissioner this DG supports.

## Role
Provides technical analysis, legal framing, and options papers to the lead Commissioner.
Participates in inter-service consultations. Does not hold political positions.

## Key outputs
- Options papers for Commissioner decision-making
- Technical sections of impact assessments
- ISC opinions on proposals from other DGs
- Implementation guidance and interpretative notices
- Infringement monitoring reports
